Who Does What?

During the onboarding process, there are many players who play a part in ensuring the new colleague’s experience is successful. Here is a breakdown of the roles and responsibilities.

You

  • Complete all assigned onboarding tasks
  • Complete all mandatory learning
  • Engage fully in the onboarding process and asks lots of questions

Manager

  • Executes all required tasks in Workday throughout the recruitment process Ensures new colleague is set up to begin work prior to their first day (equipment, workstation, system credentials, etc.)
  • Identifies a “BUDDY” to partner up with the new colleague and act as a go-to Models CORE Values and Culture Principles
  • Ensures the new colleague completes required learning including all mandatory compliance training
  • Provides ongoing feedback and engagement opportunities to new colleague (team events, performance conversations, coffee chats, etc.)

Talent Acquisition Specialist

  • Manages the interview and hiring process
  • Extends the offer letter and receives the signed offer to process in Workday
  • Completes all reference and background checks
  • Sends first day details to new colleague

Buddy

  • Acts as an orientation SUPERSTAR by helping new colleagues adjust to their new role and our company
  • Answers questions and provides guidance on the way we do things
  • Models CORE Values and Culture Principles

Orientation Team

  • Arranges new colleague’s orientation details (when and where to attend the session, what time their manager will pick them up after the session, etc.)
  • Leads the orientation session on colleague’s first day
  • Moderates the Welcome Everyone – Colleague Orientation Group on Yammer! by answering new colleague’s questions and providing engagement opportunities

HR Business Partner

  • Supports people leaders throughout the new colleague onboarding process and beyond by ensuring the program is carried out as intended
  • Provides ongoing feedback to Learning & Leadership Development regarding the orientation program
