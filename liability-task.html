First Day Activities

Online

Workday: Ensure all activities, including compliance signoffs, are complete, and all fields are populated. If any fields require your attention and you need help completing this, please speak to your manager or HR Business Partner.

You should have signed off on:

  • Code of Conduct
  • Compliance with Laws Policy
  • Violence, Harassment and Discrimination Policy
  • Alcohol and Drugs at Work Policy
  • AODA Customer Service
  • Accessibility Policy

Your manager will let you know if there are any other items you should complete based on your role. Please make sure you complete them within the given timeframe.

Learning: You must complete several eLearning courses on LVLC/SDMU your first day.

You should have signed off on:

  • Code of Conduct: eLearning (LVLC)
  • Competition Law Policy: Introduction & Sign-off
  • Respect in Our Workplace

If you are hired into a High Risk Position, you will need to complete additional training; please discuss this with your manager.

LMS: Continue to learn about the company by visiting LMS and reading a variety ofinformation about the company. We suggest you begin by reviewing some of the topics under the Life and Career section (benefits, colleague discount program, payroll) and Company section (annual CSR report, diversity and inclusion).
